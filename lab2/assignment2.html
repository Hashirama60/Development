Gol D. Roger, often referred to as "Gold Roger," was a legendary figure and the undisputed Pirate King in the world of One Piece. As the captain of the Roger Pirates, he was the only known pirate to have successfully sailed the entire Grand Line and reached the final island, Laugh Tale, where he discovered the legendary treasure known as the One Piece.

Edward Newgate, famously known as Whitebeard, was a legendary pirate and one of the Four Emperors who ruled the New World. He was renowned as "The Strongest Man in the World" following the death of the Pirate King, Gol D. Roger.

"Red-Haired" Shanks is one of the most significant and mysterious characters in the One Piece series. As the captain of the Red Hair Pirates and one of the Four Emperors, his influence on the world is immense, despite his minimal on-screen presence for much of the story.
